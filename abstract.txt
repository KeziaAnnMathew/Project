
A website for checking an individuals health based on all the factors like height, weight, cholesterol, B.P, blood sugar level etc.
Two types of roles include, users and admin, where admin is likely a health worker.
Users can register and login. They can add,edit and delete there health factors and add additional comments if any.
Admin, a Health worker, can access all users data and then assign status as either action needed or follow up.
As a future scope the admin can provide suggestions or prescription to the user based on his or her health history.
